Did you know 'Salaar' star Prabhas wanted to be a hotelier?

Prabhas has served India with one of the biggest blockbuster franchises of this era with 'Baahubali: The Beginning' and 'Baahubali: The Conclusion' and we cannot imagine Prabhas not doing what he does for a living-acting. But did you know that if Prabhas was not an actor today, he would be doing something else? Well, this superstar would be a hotelier for his love of food. Prabhas is very fond of food, and he never hesitates in exploring food to any extent, which is why a hotelier would make an apt profession for him, if not acting. However, the dashing and handsome hunk appears to be, his disinterest in acting can come as a shocker to many. Before pursuing acting, Prabhas had a different career in mind. He aspired to work in the hotel industry. Prabhas is a serious gourmet in real life. He enjoys eating food that has been prepared at home. On the sets of his movies, he regularly brings home-cooked meals for everyone.
